Industry Solutions

Intervention

The Construction Intervention Team is akin to a Special Ops Force in the Armed Services. We parachute in at the most critical time. We assess what is going well, and what needs to be fixed. We have developed a Standard Operating Policy (SOP) that details the exact checklist and guideline to right the project’s course. Our team of experts analyze the schedule, do a forensic review of the budget, observe the processes and procedures of the contractor and architect, and make meaningful recommendations to the shareholders. If need be, our Construction Intervention Team will supplement the project’s team until the project is back on track. Put our six-billion-dollars-worth of experience to work for you.

Management

PARAGON is composed of a highly skilled group of Construction Managers, Architects, Engineers, and Forensic Accountants. Our decades of experience allow us to navigate the design and construction process in a professional manner to a successful completion. Often, important leadership arrangements are delayed until many critical decisions have already been made, which can become a challenge to the project for the remainder of the job. Whether you are looking for a management team at the genesis of your project, or after setbacks have arisen, PARAGON’s Program and Construction Management Services are designed to work with your vision to deliver a completed project on time and on budget.

Consulting

Our elite staff have been sought after to help with litigation support. Once it is determined that litigation appears inevitable, it may seem like all hope is lost. However, PARAGON has worked alongside some of the largest law firms in the nation. We have secured successful outcomes even in the most difficult litigation matters. We have a long history of schedule analysis, project standard of care, forensic accounting, and many other areas of experience required for a successful completion of litigation. Our team has been tested in Mediation, Arbitration, Litigation and have always provided the highest level of integrity and professionalism. PARAGON has been instrumental in helping clients recuperate millions of dollars in damages.
